Tasmeea

AI-Powered Quran Memorization
Tasmeea helps you verify your Quran memorization using real-time validation and progressive word revelation. As you recite, words appear with color codes to notify you when making a mistake or skipping words.

Features

Real-time memorization verification
Progressive word revelation as you recite
Color-coded feedback for mistakes and skips
Complete Quran database
Smart position detection

About

Built with React Native/Expo for cross-platform mobile experience. The app uses advanced speech recognition to provide immediate feedback on your Quran recitation.

This app is free and free forever. If you want to contribute, include us in your دعاء (prayers).
