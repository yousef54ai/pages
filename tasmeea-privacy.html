Privacy Policy - Tasmeea

Last updated: September 2024

Information We Collect

Tasmeea requires access to your device's microphone to provide Quran recitation verification. We collect:

  • Audio recordings of your Quran recitation for real-time verification
  • App usage data for improving functionality

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Providing real-time Quran recitation verification
  • Converting your speech to text using Google's Speech Recognition API
  • Improving app performance and user experience

Speech Recognition

Tasmeea uses Google's Speech Recognition service to convert your recitation into text. When you speak:

  • Audio is processed by Google's servers for speech-to-text conversion
  • Only the text result is used within our app
  • Google's privacy policy applies to their speech recognition service

Data Storage

  • Audio recordings are not permanently stored on our servers
  • All Quran text data is stored locally on your device
  • Your recitation progress is saved locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

We use Google Speech Recognition API. Please review Google's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store listing.
